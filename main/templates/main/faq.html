Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

How do I place an order on SA Express?

To place an order, browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or sign in, provide shipping information, and select your payment method.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and mobile money payments. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After this time, orders are processed and cannot be changed. Contact customer service immediately if you need to make changes.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Do you offer bulk discounts for large orders?

Yes, we offer bulk discounts for orders over a certain quantity. Contact our sales team for custom pricing on large orders or corporate purchases.

What should I do if my payment is declined?

If your payment is declined, check your card details, ensure sufficient funds, and try again. If the problem persists, contact your bank or try an alternative payment method.

Can I save items for later purchase?

Yes, you can add items to your wishlist or save them in your cart for later. Items in your cart will be held for 24 hours before being released back to inventory.

How do I apply a discount code or coupon?

During checkout, you'll see a field to enter your discount code or coupon. Enter the code exactly as provided and click "Apply" to see the discount reflected in your total.

What are your shipping options and costs?

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and express shipping (2-3 business days). Shipping costs are calculated based on weight, size, and destination. Free shipping is available on orders over GHS 200.

Do you ship nationwide in Ghana?

Yes, we ship to all regions in Ghana. Delivery times may vary based on location, with major cities typically receiving faster service than remote areas.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days, express delivery takes 2-3 business days. Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day, while orders placed after 2 PM are processed the next business day.

Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

Address changes are possible within 2 hours of order placement. After this time, orders are processed and address changes may not be possible. Contact customer service immediately if needed.

What happens if I'm not available for delivery?

Our delivery partner will attempt delivery 2-3 times. If unsuccessful, the package will be held at a local pickup point for 5 business days before being returned to us.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Same-day delivery is available in selected areas of Accra and Kumasi for orders placed before 12 PM. Additional charges apply for this premium service.

How do I know if a product is in stock?

Product availability is shown on each product page. If an item is out of stock, you can sign up for notifications to be alerted when it's back in stock.

Are your products genuine and original?

Yes, all our products are 100% genuine and sourced directly from authorized distributors and manufacturers. We provide authenticity guarantees on all items.

Do you offer product warranties?

Yes, most products come with manufacturer warranties. Warranty terms vary by product and are clearly stated on each product page. We also offer extended warranty options for select items.

Can I request a product that's not in your catalog?

Yes, we accept product requests. Contact our customer service team with details about the product you're looking for, and we'll do our best to source it for you.

How often do you update your product catalog?

We regularly update our catalog with new products and remove discontinued items. New arrivals are added weekly, and seasonal collections are updated quarterly.

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" at the top of any page, fill in your details, and verify your email address. You can also create an account during the checkout process.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

How do I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team via email, phone, or live chat. Our support hours are Monday-Friday 8 AM-6 PM, and Saturday 9 AM-4 PM.

Can I update my account information?

Yes, you can update your profile information, shipping addresses, and payment methods by logging into your account and visiting the "Account Settings" section.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for most items. Products must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories. Some items like software and personal care products are non-returnable.

How do I initiate a return?

Log into your account, go to "My Orders," select the item you want to return, and follow the return process. You'll receive a return authorization number and shipping instructions.

How long do refunds take to process?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.
